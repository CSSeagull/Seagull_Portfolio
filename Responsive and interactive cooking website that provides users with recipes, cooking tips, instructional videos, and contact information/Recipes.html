One-Pot Cajun Chicken Alfredo

    Ingridients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 (8-oz.) boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning, divided
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups half-and-half, divided
  • 3 medium garlic cloves, minced (1 Tbsp.)
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 8 ounces uncooked linguine
  • 3 ounces Parmesan cheese, grated (about 3/4 cup)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley

Steps

1.Choose chicken: Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium. Season chicken with 1 tsp Cajun seasoning. Cook until browned on both sides (about 8 minutes per side). Remove and let rest for 5 minutes, then cut into cubes.

2.Prepare the sauce: In a small bowl, whisk flour with 1/4 cup half-and-half. Set aside.

3.Cook garlic and broth: Add garlic to the Dutch oven and cook for 1 minute. Add broth and remaining 1 1/4 cups half-and-half. Bring to a boil.

4.Cook pasta: Break linguine in half, add to the pot. Cover and cook for 8 minutes, stirring occasionally.

5.Combine chicken and sauce: Stir in chicken and reserved flour mixture. Cook for 2 minutes until pasta is coated and heated through.


One-Pot Cajun Chicken Alfredo

    Ingridients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 (4-oz.) boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into ½-in. strips
  • 3 small bell peppers (any color or combination), thinly sliced (about 5 cups)
  • 1 medium-size red onion
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 8 ounces of pasta
  • ⅓ cup cream cheese
  • 6 ounces pepper Jack cheese, shredded (about 1½ cups), divided
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley

Steps

1.Sear Chicken: Heat oil in a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.

2.Cook Vegetables and Spices: Add bell peppers, onion, chili powder, cumin, salt, paprika, and garlic powder. Cook for 4 minutes until peppers soften.

3.Cook Pasta: Stir in pasta and water. Bring to a boil, then cook for 12-14 minutes, stirring occasionally, until pasta is al dente and sauce thickens.

4.Preheat Broiler: Preheat oven to broil, positioning the rack 6 inches from heat.

5.Add Creamy Ingredients: Remove skillet from heat. Stir in cilantro, cream cheese, and 1 cup pepper Jack cheese until creamy.

6.Garnish and Serve: Remove from oven and garnish with cilantro. Serve hot.


Filipino New Orleans BBQ shrimp

    Ingridients

  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce (such as Crystal or Louisiana)
  • 1 tablespoon banana ketchup, such as Jufran (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic (from about 3 garlic cloves)
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh ginger (from 1 [2-inch] piece)
  • 2 pounds large deveined raw (16/20) shrimp, shell-on
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice or lemon juice
  • 1 cup (8 ounces) unsalted butter, cubed
  • 4 large scallions, finely chopped (about 3/4 cup)
  • Warm crusty baguette or cooked jasmine rice

Steps

1.Make Sauce Base: Combine beer, soy sauce, hot sauce, banana ketchup (if using), garlic, ginger, Creole seasoning, black pepper, and cayenne in a deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring, until boiling (1–2 minutes).

2.Cook Shrimp: Add shrimp and calamansi juice. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 3–5 minutes until shrimp are cooked through. Remove shrimp to a bowl.

3.Finish Sauce: Whisk in butter, one cube at a time, until melted and sauce is creamy.

4.Serve: Pour sauce over shrimp, top with scallions, and serve with a warm baguette or jasmine rice.

Ratatouille Recipe

    Ingridients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 6 roma tomatoes
  • 2 yellow squashes
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, from 8-10 leaves

Steps

1.Chopping The Veggies: Thinly slice eggplants, zucchinis, tomatoes, and squash

2.Make sauce: Sauté onions, garlic, and peppers in olive oil. Add crushed tomatoes and basil.

3.Assemble: Spread sauce in a pan, layer vegetables in a spiral pattern, and top with herb mixture (basil, thyme, parsley, garlic, olive oil).

4.Bake: Cover and bake at 375°F for 40 minutes; uncover and bake 20 minutes more.
